Our vision

Excellence and leadership in education, scientific research and community service in accordance with the requirements of local and regional quality systems and academic accreditation.

Our mission

Preparing distinguished, qualified, and specialized graduates who possess the necessary knowledge, skills, and capabilities for local and regional competition, by providing a distinct scientific and research environment that stimulates creativity and innovation with the latest means and technologies, in a way that meets the needs of society. Society and the requirements of the labor market and enhance internal and external partnership.

Our goals

  1. Linking the university with society through continuous social communication with all its institutions and sectors in terms of the university's participation in the development process in the country.
  2. Supporting and employing the scientific and human capabilities of the university to serve the community and find scientific solutions to the problems facing the community.
  3. Helping individuals acquire knowledge and skills and employ them in the development and employment of institutions.
  4. Developing continuing education programs and linking university experiences and knowledge to the scientific life of the individual and assisting him in employing the acquired experience and knowledge to increase his productivity.
  5. Spreading the concepts and policies of total quality management inside and outside the university.

Rector's message

We warmly welcome you to Alrowad University which was established as a private academic institution, striving to provide a rich and high quality learning environment and experience for students in the Republic of Yemen and the region.

We are pleased to visit the university's website, through which you will learn about this prestigious educational institution that offers high-quality and advanced programs and employs the latest tools and methodologies of higher education for its ambitious students to obtain distinguished educational opportunities.

Alrowad University operates as an academic institution with multiple goals and functions, and seeks to provide students with many educational opportunities according to their needs, backgrounds, and interests. The university offers various academic programs that contribute to the development of learners' abilities to be educated and skilled, able to assume their responsibilities in contributing to building their societies in a positive and constructive manner. The programs offered by the university include precise majors that give them knowledge and job skills on the one hand, and general culture on the other hand. Courses work in conjunction with practical training programs to prepare graduate students to be professionals, collaborative and innovative, and capable of assuming leadership roles in their organizations and fields of work.

When formulating its educational system, the university was committed to international standards, and when designing academic programs it was keen to align course vocabulary and teaching and learning methods with best practices in Arab and international universities and to respond to needs. Yemeni society and Arab society in general. Distinguished in the field of education. In order to achieve this goal, you must make an effort to excel in your pursuit of knowledge, for you will only achieve your ambition when you give your best.

Confident that your ambitions will not stop at any limits, we put in your hands all that you can benefit from the capabilities provided by the university to serve you, from carefully equipped facilities, modern and advanced scientific and training laboratories, as well as distinguished classes and professors, all in order to be supportive of you in Your way to the heights and reach the desired goals.

May God bless you and direct your steps on the paths of success.

President of Alrowad University.
